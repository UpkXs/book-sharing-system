Traditions and customs in Kazakh culture

Our national culture has absorbed the achievements of world culture and is a part and product of world history and culture. The study of the history and culture of the people is impossible without understanding the origins of ethnic development, priority spiritual and moral values of Kazakh culture. In the history of any nation, the understanding of the past lays the foundation and prospects for the development of the future. 
As President N.A. Nazarbayev notes, "... Kazakhs - heirs of great civilizations - managed to carry their national self over all historical abysses and depressions. And not only to carry, not only to save, but also to multiply"
